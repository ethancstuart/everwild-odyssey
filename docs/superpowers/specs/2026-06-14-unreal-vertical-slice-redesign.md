# Everwild Odyssey Unreal Vertical Slice Redesign

## Intent

Replace the primitive alpha presentation with a playable Unreal vertical slice that reads as a heroic, MMO-scale fantasy valley from the first camera frame. The slice remains single-player, controller-friendly, and runtime-authored so it can be launched immediately without imported marketplace assets.

## Player Fantasy

The player arrives as a Runeblade champion at Starfall Vale, a Dawnwatch frontier zone under relic surge. The first playable loop is: orient in a large valley, accept the Starfall Gate quest, fight a clustered relic surge, collect enough shards through combat, and stabilize the gate.

## Acceptance

- The world scaffold presents an authored alpha valley with many named landmarks, scenic props, atmospheric lights, water, roadways, ruins, forests, crystals, and a distant keep.
- The opening encounter has enough enemies to feel like a zone event, while the first target remains within immediate attack range.
- Hero presentation includes visible party companions, not only data records.
- The HUD shows vitals, progression, active objective, world event status, controls, and controller mappings.
- Combat advances the quest loop by rewarding XP and relic-shard progress, and resolving the event after enough defeated enemies.
- The project compiles, launches standalone, and opens to the upgraded OpenWorld slice.
